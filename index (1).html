The system

Six steps.
Fully automated.

01
Your list goes in. That's the last thing you do manually.

Drop your contacts into a spreadsheet — name, role, company, any context you have. The system reads it and takes it from there. No CRM. No imports. No reformatting required.

Google Sheets · CSV
02
It knows exactly who's already been contacted.

Before touching a single contact, the system cross-checks every previous send. Already emailed? Skip. Waiting on a reply? Skip. Sending the same email twice isn't a risk — it's impossible.

Zero duplicates — always
03
AI writes a real email. Not a template with brackets.

Using their name, title, and company, the AI composes a short, natural-sounding message — the kind a thoughtful person would write after doing their homework. Every contact gets something written just for them.

Unique per contact · AI-written
04
Sent automatically — with pacing that looks human.

Emails go out with randomized delays between each send — not a fixed interval, but a varied rhythm that email providers can't flag as automation. Daily send limits protect your domain for the long term.

Random delays · Daily cap
05
No reply? A follow-up lands on its own.

Most deals close on the second or third touch. SendFlow knows this. Contacts who haven't replied get a follow-up at your configured interval — automatically queued, automatically sent. Not a single lead falls through.

Auto follow-up · Configurable timing

Bulletproof deduplication

Tracks every sent email in a persistent log. Re-run the system 10 times — it will never email the same person twice. Your list stays clean, your reputation stays intact.

Randomized send timing

Sends at irregular intervals that mimic a real person's behavior. No predictable patterns for spam filters to catch. Better deliverability, by design — not by luck.

Set a max per day. Hit it and the system pauses, picks up tomorrow. Your sending volume stays within the bounds that keep your domain healthy for the long haul.
